Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use both Featured Profile and Profile Highlighter at the same time?

Yes! You can absolutely use both services simultaneously for maximum visibility. Many of our most successful users combine both features to get the benefits of top placement in search results along with the visual highlighting that makes their profile stand out in listings.

Will this guarantee me a job?

While we can't guarantee you'll get a job (that ultimately depends on your qualifications and interview performance), our visibility features significantly increase your chances by ensuring more employers see your profile. On average, users of these features receive 3-5x more interview requests.

How do I remove the highlight or featured status?

You can remove these features at any time through your account settings. Simply go to the "Profile Visibility" section and toggle off the features you no longer want. Any remaining time on your subscription will be paused in case you want to reactivate later.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the results. Just contact our support team within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.

How long does it take to see results?

Most users see increased profile views within 24-48 hours, though the exact timing depends on when employers in your field are actively searching for candidates. We recommend keeping the features active for at least 2 weeks to maximize your visibility.

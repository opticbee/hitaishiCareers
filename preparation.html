Interview Preparation

Behavioral Interviews

Master the STAR technique

  • Situation: Set the context
  • Task: Describe your responsibility
  • Action: Explain what you did
  • Result: Share the outcome

Technical Interviews

Prepare for coding challenges

  • Review data structures & algorithms
  • Practice on coding platforms
  • Think out loud during solving
  • Ask clarifying questions

Virtual Interviews

Tips for remote interviews

  • Test your tech setup beforehand
  • Choose a quiet, well-lit space
  • Dress professionally (at least top half)
  • Maintain eye contact with the camera
